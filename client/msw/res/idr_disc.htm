We Respect Your Privacy

Pueblo collects usage statistics so we can serve you better. Specifically, Pueblo tracks the following things:

  • What worlds you connect to.
  • The time that you connect to a world.
  • The time that you disconnect from a world.
  • The version of the Pueblo/UE client you are using.

That's it. We protect your privacy. Pueblo does not log your MUD account ids or anything else you type into a MUD.

We do not store any information that can be traced to you personally or to your computer. There is also no way to associate your registration details with the worlds you connect to. We will never sell or give away your personal details -- we hate spam just as much as you do!
